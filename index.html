The project consists of four SQL queries, aimed at extracting specific data from an employee database. The queries focus on analyses related to employee gender, managers, salaries, and the application of a stored procedure for calculating the average salary by gender and department.

REAL ESTATE MARKET ANALYSIS

This project aims to provide a comprehensive analysis of the housing market in Greece during 2022 by examining historical data, identifying trends, and making predictions about future price movements. By leveraging SQL for data extraction and descriptive analysis, followed by regression analysis in R, this study will offer valuable insights into the factors driving market fluctuations and assist stakeholders in making informed decisions.
